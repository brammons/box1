President's Day Weekend:

Tahoe Edition

We saw Sacramento and Tahoe together for President's Day Weekend. The trip was February 16th to February 19th. There were some worries about the snow storm(s) coming into Tahoe around the same time that we were headed up there, so we lost a few soldiers, but overall, it was an amazing trip. Our faithful drivers, Kenny & Carol, got us there and back in one piece--some of us a little scratched up by the end, but one piece nonetheless.
